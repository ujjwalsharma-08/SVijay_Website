Field Visits

Himalayan Glacier Expedition 2024

Location: Chorabari Glacier, Uttarakhand • Date: May 2024

This field visit focused on in-situ glacier mass balance measurements, installation of time-lapse cameras, and snow/ice sampling for remote sensing validation. The team trekked to the glacier snout and set up monitoring equipment for the melt season.

Greenland Supraglacial Lake Survey

Location: Kangerlussuaq, Greenland • Date: July 2023

This expedition involved supraglacial lake mapping, drone surveys, and water sampling to study lake drainage events and their impact on ice flow. The team collaborated with local researchers and collected valuable field data for satellite validation.

Projects & Grants

Supraglacial lakes & ice velocity coupling, Greenland

Role: PI • Funding: ISRO • Duration: 2022–2025

This project investigates the coupling between supraglacial lakes and ice velocity in Greenland using remote sensing and field data. The aim is to understand how surface meltwater influences glacier dynamics and contributes to mass loss.

High-resolution Himalayan glacier monitoring

Role: PI • Funding: IIT Roorkee (FIG) • Duration: 2022–2024

This project focuses on high-resolution monitoring of Himalayan glaciers using satellite imagery and field campaigns, aiming to quantify glacier changes and their response to climate variability.

Glacier change classification – Uttarakhand

Role: Indian PI • Funding: Bavarian Indian Center • Duration: 2021

This project classifies glacier changes in Uttarakhand using multi-source satellite data, providing insights into regional glacier health and supporting water resource management.
